Pasta Carbonara

Description

A classic italian dish combining salt and creamy flavours with a touch of black pepper.

Perfect weeknight dinner that you can make in only 20 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 150g Bacon or Pancetta
  • 1 dl Parmecan Cheese
  • A pinch of Black Pepper
  • 2 servings of Spagetthi
  • 4 eggs

Steps

  1. Cook your pasta according to the package instructions. You want to take it out just before finished since we're going to cook it a bit more later.
  2. Fry bacon starting from a cold pan. Cook until crispy then remove from heat.
  3. Mix 4 egg yolks with cheese and black pepper
  4. In the same pan as you cooked your bacon, add your finished pasta and your cheese and egg mixture.
  5. Add pasta water until you get the consistency you want.
  6. Add bacon, serve with cheese and black pepper
  7. Enjoy!
