Pla Lad Prik

Description

A spicy dish with fish at it's focus. The spicy coriander heavy sour sauce is the perfect mix!

Ingredients

  • Fried fish, I like the store bought ones
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • 5 thai chillies
  • 1 dl of fine chopped Coriander
  • 4 tsp Tamarind cooking sauce
  • 2 tsp Palm sugar
  • A splash of fish sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the fish according to the packet.
  2. In a blender, roughly chop up the garlic and chillies. Add the coriander.
  3. Fry the garlic mix in a splash of oil until fragrant.
  4. Add palm sugar and tamarind sauce.
  5. Add water until your desired concistency.
  6. Add fish sauce to taste.
  7. Enjoy!
